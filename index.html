sustainability doesn't have to be expensive!



sustainability isn't about buying all the fancy zero-waste products, it's about using what you already have at home! it's about giving that plastic container an extra life before you recycle it, buying less stuff that you don't need, and really thinking about ways you can conserve resources like water in your life, by doing what works for you! if you have the means to invest in sustainable products, go ahead! but if you don't, it's totally okay too.
